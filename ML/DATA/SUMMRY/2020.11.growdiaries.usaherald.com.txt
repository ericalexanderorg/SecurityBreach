Researcher Volodymyr "Bob" Diachenko reported a. data breach linked to GrowDiaries. In his November 3 report, he claimed that private data including passwords, posts, email addresses, and IP addresses were exposed between September 22 and October 15. GrowDiaries is an online community and journaling platform designed to support and advise marijuana growers. After reporting the vulnerability, GrowDiaries asked for additional details and by Oct. 15, the data has been secured, he added. For the GrowDiaries community, passwords must be changed as soon as possible. A representative from GrowDiaries disputed Diachenko's report. In an email, asserting that the company "Never acknowledged the incident" and that the data that was allegedly compromised was only test data.
