A ransomware attack hit TFI International's four Canadian courier divisions on Thursday, two days after the transportation and logistics company raised millions of dollars in a share offering. The attack impacted "Some systems" of Canpar Express, ICS Courier, Loomis Express and TForce Integrated Solutions, according to notices posted to the couriers' websites. A spokesperson for TFI International did not immediately respond to FreightWaves' request for comment. Ransomware attacks typically involve hackers locking down systems and demanding money to restore access. TFI told its customers that the attack is under investigation.
