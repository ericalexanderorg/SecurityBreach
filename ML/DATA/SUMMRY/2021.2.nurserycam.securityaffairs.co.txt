Daycare camera product NurseryCam was hacked last week, the company was forced to shut down its IoT camera service. On Friday, The Register become aware of the compromise of the NurseryCam network. NurseryCam is produced by the companies FootfallCam Ltd and Meta Technologies Ltd. In response to the incident, the company shut down its IoT camera service on Saturday and reported the security breach to the parents. "On 17:18 Friday 19th February 2021, it has come to our attention of a cyber incident detected in our NurseryCam system." reads the security noticed sent by the company to the parents. The service was used by about 40 nurseries across the UK..
