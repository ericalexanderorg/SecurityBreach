Attorneys working with ClassAction.org are looking into whether a class action lawsuit can be filed in light of the Century Federal Credit Union data breach. Century Federal Credit Union has notified consumers about a recent incident where an unauthorized party gained access to an employee account between August 30, 2023 and September 30, 2023. Approximately 13,984 individuals were affected by this breach. If your information was exposed in the breach, attorneys want to hear from you. You may be able to start a class action lawsuit to recover compensation for loss of privacy, time spent dealing with the breach, out-of-pocket costs, and more. A successful case could also force Century Federal Credit Union to ensure it takes proper steps to protect the information it was entrusted with.
