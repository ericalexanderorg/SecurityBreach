The 125-year conglomerate did not have a groupwide disaster recovery plan, and its backup process was fragmented. "In short, we centralized IT, and backup was one of the key things that we centralized," said Chan. Chan spent a year convincing his senior management to invest in proper disaster recovery and backup. While the company survived the attack, it put the spotlight on having clean backup copies. Jebsen Group used Veeam Availability Suite and Veeam Backup for Microsoft Office 365 to restore critical data with four hours. The simplicity of the system allowed the conglomerate to backup every hour. The ease of use and the use of a single tool for backup and disaster recovery also reduced training matters.
