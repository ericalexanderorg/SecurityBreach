Hendersonville, a city in North Carolina, has faced a significant cyberattack. The attack targeted employee data management software. Suspected threat actors may have exposed employee data, mainly affecting those hired before January 1, 2021. The city is actively investigating with cybersecurity experts and law enforcement. This includes collaboration with the North Carolina Joint Cybersecurity Task Force and third-party cybersecurity specialists. North Carolina's unique stance against ransomware includes a ban on ransom payments to hackers. Hendersonville plans to contact individuals affected by the data breach.
